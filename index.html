One yard at a time.

We are restoring Californian cities to diverse ecosystems, by bringing plants, insects and wildlife to urban areas where they no longer exist. Cities are dominated by just a few invasive species that are not suited to the environment of California today. Our approach is a hybrid of ecological restoration, plant-insect ecology and modern, sustainable landscape design.
